Lockers

Level 3, Lot 27 (GSC Cinema)
Token Machine only accept RM5, RM10 & Rm20 cash note)

T&C Apply:

Lost Key: RM50 fine will be imposed.

Safety Note:
• You are not encouraged to keep valuable/pet animals/digital items inside the locker for safety reasons.

Usage Mechanics:
STEP 1 - Exchange cash notes to tokens by using the Token Machine.
STEP 2 - Insert token into the door lock, put your items inside the locker and close the door.
STEP 3 - Turn the key to the left and pull out the key.
